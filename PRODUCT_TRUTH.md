# Sent - Product Truth (Canonical)

## What we are
Sent is a premium AI tool that helps people send better, more personal messages for important moments.

## What we are not
- Not a CRM
- Not a social network
- Not an automation platform

## Core loop
Contact -> Occasion -> Generate -> Postcard

## Pricing

Free:
- 3 messages / month
- 10 contacts

Sent+ ($4.99/mo):
- Unlimited messages
- Full AI generation
- All voices
- Full postcard builder

Pro ($12.99/mo):
- Future tier (do not oversell)

## Messaging pillars
- Show up better
- Don't miss the moment
- Sounds like you
- Make it something they keep
